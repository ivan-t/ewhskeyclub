Membership

Why should you join?

You can earn service/CAS hours, make new friends, and go to district conventions filled with other enthusiastic students. Being passionate and actively involved about Key Club impresses colleges and earns you scholarships. By making a difference in other people's lives, Key Club members build both their character and leadership skills.

What kinds of things do we do?

We bake pies for a local church's Thanksgiving dinner, volunteer at events like March of Dimes and the NAMI walk, help out at weekly church dinners, host the "Redeeming Soles" shoe drive, set up an Easter egg hunt in Edmonds, and do fundraisers for our district project (KCCP) and DCON!

How do I join?

Come to our next meeting (every Tuesday), pay your membership due ($18.00) before October 4th, and ask one of the officers about Key Club!

When and where are the meetings?

We meet on Tuesdays in the F100 flex at 7:00-8:00 PM!

Membership Requirements

In order to be a Key Club member, you must be active! We understand that students have other commitments such as sports and music, however in order to be considered an active member in our Key Club they must meet our requirements. These requirements include:

  • Attending most weekly meetings and stay updated with current service events
  • Have a minimum of 10 service hours per semester
  • 15 hours minimum for those who want to attend DCON
  • Be passionate about Key Club! Caring is our way of life
